About

The Kenyatta International Convention Centre (KICC) is one of the most iconic buildings in Nairobi and a premier venue for conferences, exhibitions, and events in East Africa. Located in the heart of the city, KICC offers world-class facilities including a plenary hall seating 6,000, multiple breakout rooms, and a helipad with panoramic rooftop views of Nairobi. The venue features modern conferencing technology, dedicated event support staff, and in-house catering from award-winning chefs specializing in both local and international cuisine.
